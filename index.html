About company

“Kepil Hil” Company has extensive experience in providing services and spare parts for all types of transport.

“Kepil Hil” Economic Society is the part of the group of companies “Altyn Taslama” Economic Society, “Polat Hyzmat” Economic Society, “Begenchli Yol” Economic Society, “Bash Taraply Birleshme” Economic Society.

The main principle of our company is to be a reliable contractor, we guarantee the stability, reliability and high quality of the services provided, a high level of technical equipment, which allows you to carry out the entire range of repairs of vehicles with proper quality and in proper time.
